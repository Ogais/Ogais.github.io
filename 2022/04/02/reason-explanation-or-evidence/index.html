#7 Reasons: Explanations or Evidence?

-

eg.推倒：解释性理由/规范性理由

reason：1不可解释；2“应当做某事”的解释（by John Broome）；3应当做某事的证据，而不是解释

对Broome的第一眼优势的接受；弱点

证据/解释：

F解释G：F makes it the case that G. F is why G is the case.
G must be the case.

F是G的证据：F indicates (or at least makes it more epistemically probable) that G is true.
G may not obtain.

F explains G $\rightarrow$ F is evidence that G , 反之？

REASONS AS EXPLANATIONS

why N ought to do F == what makes it the case that N ought to do F

perfect reason / pro tantos reason / reason

RP:

A perfect reason for N to F is an explanation of why N ought to F.

perfect reason, conclusive reason, canonical reason

eg. 看牙医-为什么要去找他？
完美理由：“他是最好的医生” / “我应该去看最好的医生”x
sufficient explanations for why you ought to do a particular act
决定性理由：“他是最好的医生” + “我应该去看最好的医生”
sufficient conditions for the obtaining of the ought fact in particular contexts
Canonical reason: 如，经济困窘——解释为什么应该去看最好的医生
sufficient conditions for obtaining ought fact in all contexts

RT:

A pro tanto reason for N to F is whatever plays the for-F role in a weighing explanation of why N ought to F, or in a weighing explanation of why N ought not to F,
or in a weighing explanation of why it is not the case that N ought to F and not the case that N ought not to F.

不止是第一个：天平？两段无法比较？

RD

A reason for N to F is either RP or RT.

优点

1、analysis of practical reasons in other terms；（我们的

2、role to make it the case（唯一Broome承认的

3、 Finally, this account of reasons provides room for there to be reasons that are not considerations that need to be weighed against each other.
为一些不需要和其他理由衡量的理由留出空间
（？？？？？真的是positive feature 吗

​ eg1. rigid deontic rules: reasons that have infinite weight（怀疑
​ eg2. evidentialism, reason not to believe contradictions that can’t be weigh against others（不承认no contradiction belief

REASONS AS EVIDENCE

优势-广域reason：行动和信念，实践和理论

eg.公交车
evidence：车来了，他意识到了，他上班快迟到了……
evidence that one ( ought to ) do / not do sth.

R: Necessarily, a fact X is a reason for an agent N to F if and only if (iff) X is evidence that N ought to F.
RB: Necessarily, X is a reason for an agent N to believe that P iff X is evidence that N ought to believe that P.

存在/拥有：

证据和理由的映射
reason for N / reason that N has

优点

1、analysis of practical reasons in other terms；（共有的？
2、行动-信仰，扩展
3、简洁性……

比较

it better explains the practical role of reasons (Sec. III.A), it is the only account that can actually provide a
full-blooded analysis of what it is to be a reason (Sec. III.B), and it is a more simple and elegant account Sec. III.C).

B

RP：
强度太大：if one has a reason to F, then one ought to F; reason is explanation; if one has an explanation of why one ought to F, one ought to F. – no pro tanto merely

RT：
A pro tanto reason for N to F is whatever plays the for-F role in a weighing explanation of why N ought to F, or in a weighing explanation of why N ought not to F,
or in a weighing explanation of why it is not the case that N ought to F and not the case that N ought not to F.
有的事实支持F，有的反对。
权衡解释中事实的for-F role: 该事实in favor of F。
问题：for-F role和reason的……同义反复？
回应：for-F role就是在权衡解释中胜出的那一边。
反驳：“胜出”的确定依靠“in favor of”

缺乏强度分析：拒斥理由是某种权重？–绝对的，还是相对的？

ought & reason

1、one ought to F → reason for one to F
2、reason – evidence
3、one ought to F → evidence that one ought to F（诶这同时也是一种削弱后件……

问题：3。一定有F为真的证据吗？
相比，Broome不受到此攻击。

回应：
承认one ought to F → there is evidence that one ought to F：存在这个证据，而不是某人有这个证据
且，one ought to F → knowable evidence that one ought to F：只有可知，才是证据。

回应2：
Broome的攻击。
1、如果，one ought to F → there is a reason to F（他承认这一点吗？？？
则，one ought to F → there is an explanation of why one ought to F——承认对每一个事实存在解释
！：fundamental facts (eg. 功利最大化)没有解释
2、如果，explanation ( evidence
则，one ought to F → evidence that one ought to F——无法逃脱攻击
！：evidence !( explanation，Broome承诺的更强
